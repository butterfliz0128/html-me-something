My journey into the world of Information Technology ...

My education started in the small town of Dixon, Missouri at Dixon High School. I have always enjoyed school and learning. My main interests were in the areas of math and science, so I knew I wanted to find a career focused along those lines. Having an older sister who worked in what was called "Data Processing" back in those days convinced me to pursue a degree in Computer Science. She lived in St. Louis, so I moved up to the big city (STL) to go to college at University of Missouri - St. Louis. When I got there, I realized it was more of a business school, so I decided to change to Business Administration with an emphasis in Management Information Systems and Finance.

My sophomore year, I landed a co-op job with McDonnell Douglas. This was the start of my IT Career. Before my co-op gig ended, I was hired as a full-time employee. I have been a business programmer analyst for 3 major companies for 20+ years. Most of experience is with COBOL and the mainframe which is why I decided to do LaunchCode - want to learn web development!
